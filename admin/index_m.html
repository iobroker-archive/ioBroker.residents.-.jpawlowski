    Activity Number Reference
    • expand_more
      0-999: Not present at home / Away
      • 0: Extended Away
      • 1: Away
      • 2: Way Home
      • 3-99: [Reserved for other away states]
      • 100-199: Default Focus Modes during absence
      • 200-299: Custom Focus Modes during absence
      • 300-999: [Reserved]
    • expand_more
      1.000-1.999: WAKING TIME at home
      • 1000: Home
      • 1001-1099: [Reserved for other home states]
      • 1100-1199: Default Focus Modes during presence
      • 1200-1299: Custom Focus Modes during presence
      • 1300-1899: [Reserved]
      • 1900-1999: Transitioning to Sleeping Time
      • 1900: Wind Down / Preparing Bedtime
      • 1901: Bedtime / Getting to Bed
      • 1902: Bedtime / In Bed
      • 1903-1999: [Reserved for other bedtime states]
    • expand_more
      2.000-2.999: SLEEPING TIME at home
      • 2000: Night
      • 2010-2099: While I should be sleeping
      • 2010: Awake during Night Time
      • 2020: Asleep again
      • 2100-2199: While I should get up
      • 2100: Wake-up Alarm
      • 2101: Wake-up Alarm - 1 x Snooze
      • 2102: Wake-up Alarm - 2 x Snooze
      • 2103: Wake-up Alarm - 3 x Snooze
      • 2104: Wake-up Alarm - 4 x Snooze
      • 2105: Wake-up Alarm - 5+ x Snooze
      • 2200-2299: Transitioning to Waking Time
      • 2200: Awakening by Wake-up Alarm
      • 2210: Awakening by presence state change
    • expand_more
      10.000-12.999: Do Not Disturb
      Each number from 0-2999 is added 10.000 to represent the same status when Do Not Disturb is enabled.
